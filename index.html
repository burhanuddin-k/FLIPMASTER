Why FlipMaster

A coin flip you can actually trust

Most "flip a coin" tools are a single button on a blank page. FlipMaster treats the flip as the whole product: a fair, verifiable random result, a history you can review, and statistics that hold you accountable to your own calls.

Whether you're settling a debate, making a low-stakes decision, or just testing your luck against the streak counter, every flip is logged, timestamped, and yours to look back on.

Questions

Frequently asked

Is the flip actually random?

Yes. Each flip uses a uniform random draw, so heads and tails each land at roughly 50% over enough flips.

Is my flip history saved?

Yes — every flip is stored so your statistics and recent history persist between visits.

Can I clear my history?

Yes, use the "Clear history" button above the history panel. This resets your stored flips.

Does it work on mobile?

FlipMaster is fully responsive with dedicated layouts for phone, tablet, and desktop.

Can I use the keyboard instead of tapping?

Press the Space bar at any time to trigger a flip, no mouse needed.
